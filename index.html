I am Darwin

Dawn is coming Open your eyes

Hi, I'm Darwin, currently a Psychology undergraduate at Peking University.

Grew up in China, think in Chinese, write in English, code in whatever the problem wants. What I keep coming back to is how people decide, remember and connect—the kind of questions psychology asks when it gets serious about data. When I'm not at a desk I tend to end up on a road: I've ridden across more Chinese provinces than I can list from memory, and once pedalled my way out of the country and into Kazakhstan. I've finished a full marathon, train in MMA and Sanda, and will show up for almost any ball sport. I build things with AI that I actually use—some of them end up being genuinely useful, most of them at least teach me something new. I also make a lot of visual work: graphic design, promotional materials, photography, DIY postcards, and video I edit myself. Most of it lives in the portfolio below.
